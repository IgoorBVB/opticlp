About

Founded in 2021 following the merger of OpTic Gaming and Envy Gaming, OpTic Texas is competing in the 2022 Call of Duty League and looks to reclaim the title that was won by the Dallas Empire in the Inaugural Season of the COD League in 2020.

Representing OpTic Gaming and the passionate OpTic fanbase known as The GREENWALL, OpTic Texas, led by current OpTic Gaming President, Hector “H3CZ” Rodriguez, enters the 2022 Call of Duty League season with a star-studded roster featuring Call of Duty legend, Seth “Scump” Abner alongside Multi-FPS Champion, Anthony “Shotzzy” Cuevas-Castro, Indervir “Illey” Dhaliwal, and Brandon “Dashy” Otell.
